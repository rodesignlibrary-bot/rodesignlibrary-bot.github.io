📖 UF System Design Rules

Membrane Parameters
• Hollow Fiber (HF): most common, 40–80 LMH operating flux
• Spiral Wound: lower flux 20–40 LMH, for high-TSS feeds
• Tubular: highest flux 80–150 LMH, easy cleaning
• Module area: typically 50, 80, or 100 M²
• Max 60 modules per skid/stream
Backwash Standards
• BW Flux: 100–150 LMH (typically 2× operating)
• BW Duration: 3–10 min
• BW Interval: 20–60 min
• BW Pressure: 1.5–4.0 bar
• Always 1+1 duty/standby for critical service
Air Scour
• Air rate: 8–15 Nm³/hr per module
• Blower pressure: 0.3–0.7 bar
• Simultaneous air+water BW recommended
CIP & Recovery
• System recovery: 85–95% typical
• CIP every 30–90 days depending on feed quality
• CIP chemicals: NaOCl (0.5–1%), NaOH (0.1 M), HCl (0.5%)
• CIP tank sized for 1–2× module volume
